# Leaf Green Training Solutions - Project Outline

## File Structure

**Main Files**:
- `index.html` - Cover page with hero section and navigation
- `about.html` - Company mission and values
- `audit.html` - Anonymous Performance Audit demonstration
- `services.html` - Comprehensive training offerings
- `process.html` - 5-stage methodology visualization
- `trackrecord.html` - Client portfolio and success stories
- `cta.html` - Call to action and contact information

**Assets**:
- `main.js` - Interactive functionality and animations
- `resources/` - Image assets and media files

### 1. Cover Page (index.html)
- Hero section with compelling visual

- Navigation to other sections

- Company mission and philosophy
- Market positioning statement

### 3. Anonymous Performance Audit (audit.html)

### 4. Why Choose Leaf Green (whyus.html)

### 5. Services (services.html)
- Comprehensive training catalog

### 6. Process (process.html)

### 7. Track Record (trackrecord.html)
