# Leaf Green Training Solutions - Design Strategy

## Design Philosophy

**Premium Corporate Authority**: The design embodies sophisticated business consulting aesthetics, positioning Leaf Green as the premium training provider in Botswana. We're creating a visual language that speaks to C-suite executives and decision-makers.

**Color Palette**: 
- Primary: Deep Forest Green (#1B4332) - Authority, growth, stability
- Secondary: Sage Green (#52B788) - Fresh thinking, innovation
- Accent: Charcoal (#2D3748) - Professional depth
- Neutral: Warm White (#FEFEFE) - Clarity, openness
- Text: Rich Black (#1A202C) for maximum readability

**Typography**:
- Display: Tiempos Headline - Bold, authoritative serif for headings
- Body: Suisse Int'l - Clean, modern sans-serif for content
- Accent: Used sparingly for emphasis and calls-to-action

## Visual Language

**Layout Strategy**: Magazine-style editorial design with generous white space, creating breathing room that signals confidence and premium positioning.

**Image Treatment**: High-contrast, professional photography with subtle green overlays to maintain brand cohesion while preserving image clarity.

**Interactive Elements**: Sophisticated hover states and micro-animations that feel premium, not flashy - reinforcing the professional positioning.

## Section Strategy

1. **Cover Page**: Hero-focused with compelling visual hierarchy
2. **About Us**: Mission-driven with supporting imagery
3. **Anonymous Audit**: Interactive demonstration of the proprietary process
4. **Why Choose Us**: Benefits-focused with data visualization
5. **Services**: Comprehensive offerings with visual categorization
6. **Process**: Step-by-step visual workflow
7. **Track Record**: Client showcase with credibility indicators
8. **Call to Action**: Conversion-focused with clear next steps

## Technical Approach

**Framework**: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript with premium animation libraries
**Performance**: Optimized for fast loading and smooth interactions
**Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and keyboard navigation support
**Print-Ready**: CSS designed for clean PDF conversion
